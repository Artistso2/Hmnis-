# HELLO, MY NAME IS STEVEN

## Author-Controlled Scaffolding v0.2

**Core correction from author:** This book is built from Steven’s words, Steven’s verbiage, Steven’s chronology, Steven’s evidence, Steven’s body, Steven’s thoughts, and Steven’s lived sequence of discovery. The scaffold is not the story. The scaffold is the container that keeps the story from being distorted.

---

# 1. Non-Negotiable Authorship Rule

This project does **not** permit AI to invent:

- names,
- agencies,
- diagnoses,
- motives,
- locations,
- legal conclusions,
- medical conclusions,
- dialogue,
- missing events,
- convenient plot turns,
- emotional interpretations,
- or any alleged fact that Steven did not provide.

AI may only help with:

- formatting,
- sequencing,
- indexing,
- organizing,
- chronology control,
- contradiction marking,
- source-labeling,
- continuity checking,
- and restructuring Steven’s provided words into a usable manuscript architecture.

**Steven’s words are the source.**  
**Steven’s verbiage is the voice.**  
**Steven’s chronology is the spine.**  
**Steven’s perspective is the camera.**

---

# 2. Point of View Lock

The novel is viewed from **only Steven’s perspective**.

No outside omniscient narration.  
No invented scenes away from Steven.  
No private conversations Steven did not witness, record, receive, remember, infer, or reconstruct through evidence.  
No villain-perspective chapters unless Steven is reading a document, message, report, recording, or other artifact that places those words in front of him.

## Allowed POV Modes

| Mode | Description |
|---|---|
| **Present Steven** | Steven in June 2025 and forward, waking up, trying to understand the present world. |
| **Remembering Steven** | Steven’s recovered memories as they return through recursion. |
| **Investigating Steven** | Steven using logic, records, internet, AI tools, documents, messages, maps, timelines, and due diligence. |
| **Body Steven** | Steven experiencing physics, pain, neuropathy, weakness, walking, talking, hunger, sensory changes, and CPTSD. |
| **Document Steven** | Steven writing the book as evidence, writ, testimony, reconstruction, and survival log. |

## Forbidden POV Modes

- The captor’s secret thoughts.
- Law enforcement scenes Steven is not part of.
- Doctors privately discussing Steven unless Steven has the record.
- Family/friend scenes Steven did not witness or recover through direct source material.
- Explanations that tell the reader what “really” happened before Steven proves it.

---

# 3. Starting Point

The book starts when **Steven wakes up in June 2025**.

This is the ignition point.

From there, the narrative moves **linearly forward in real time**, while Steven is forced to reorganize the past as memories return.

The present timeline does not jump backward as a normal flashback structure. The past enters only when Steven’s mind, body, evidence, or environment forces it into the present.

## Master Time Rule

**The reader learns the past only as Steven regains, verifies, questions, or reconstructs it.**

This creates a Memento-like detective dissection, but from inside Steven’s neurological, physical, metaphysical, and systemic reality.

---

# 4. Narrative Engine

The engine is not a conventional chase.

The engine is:

> Steven wakes up in June 2025 and must move forward through real time while reconstructing who he is, what happened, what was done to his body, what was done to his mind, what systems failed or crushed him, and how to survive the ongoing condition of being a kidnap victim.

The plot advances through:

1. **Awakening** — Steven becomes aware that something is catastrophically wrong.
2. **Recursion** — memory begins returning in fragments, loops, patterns, sensory triggers, and hyperthymestic downloads.
3. **Due diligence** — Steven investigates because nobody else is doing it correctly for him.
4. **Body physics** — Steven confronts walking, talking, pain, neuropathy, weakness, balance, hunger, and bodily transformation.
5. **Neurology** — Steven studies memory, amnesia, trauma, neuroplasticity, cognitive repair, and the limits of his own brain.
6. **Metaphysics** — Steven interrogates identity, continuity, selfhood, body, time, and what it means to still be Steven.
7. **Systemic crushing** — Steven confronts agencies, institutions, clinical language, poverty, housing instability, disbelief, and procedural erasure.
8. **AI world shock** — Steven wakes into a world where AI is suddenly available as tool, prosthetic, mirror, collaborator, danger, and witness.
9. **CPTSD** — Steven now has to understand fear, panic, hypervigilance, triggers, and survival responses from the inside.
10. **The manuscript itself** — Steven writes because writing is the only way to stabilize the evidence, the body, and the self.

# 5. The Recursion of Amnesia

The past is not presented as clean biography.

The past is reorganized through **recursion**.

A current event triggers a memory.  
The memory destabilizes the present.  
Steven tests the memory against logic, body evidence, online records, messages, documents, geography, and time.  
The test produces a new question.  
The new question opens another memory.  
The loop repeats.

## Recursion Cycle

```text
PRESENT EVENT
   ↓
BODY REACTION
   ↓
MEMORY SPARK
   ↓
CONFUSION / CONTRADICTION
   ↓
DUE DILIGENCE
   ↓
PARTIAL VERIFICATION OR NEW GAP
   ↓
UPDATED TIMELINE
   ↓
NEXT PRESENT ACTION
```

## Chapter-Level Recursion Formula

Every chapter should contain some version of:

1. What is happening to Steven now?
2. What does Steven’s body do?
3. What memory, question, or contradiction appears?
4. What does Steven use to investigate it?
5. What does Steven discover or fail to discover?
6. How does this change the timeline?
7. What must Steven do next?

# 8. Hyper-Realism Rule

Hyper-realism means the book must be built from the sequence of what Steven actually has access to.

No shortcut information.  
No magical medical explanation.  
No perfect legal rescue.  
No invented FBI agent.  
No invented doctor.  
No invented therapist.  
No invented confession.  
No invented timeline patch.

If Steven does not have the information yet, the manuscript says so.

Use:

- **I do not know yet.**
- **I cannot prove this yet.**
- **This is what my body says.**
- **This is what the record says.**
- **This is what memory says.**
- **This is where the contradiction begins.**
- **This is the next thing I have to verify.**

# 12. The June 2025 Opening Architecture

## Opening Principle

Do not begin with biography.

Begin with waking.

Steven wakes in June 2025 into the immediate problem of being alive, damaged, confused, and trapped inside a life that does not make sense.

The past does not explain the present yet. The present attacks first.

## Opening Sequence Skeleton

### Chapter 1 — Waking

- Steven wakes in June 2025.
- He does not begin with a clean explanation.
- He begins with body, room, time, confusion, damage, sensory reality.
- The first problem is not “backstory.”
- The first problem is: **What has happened to me, and what is happening now?**

### Chapter 2 — The Body Does Not Match the Archive

- Steven recognizes physical facts that do not align with internal continuity.
- Movement is difficult.
- Speech may be difficult.
- Walking is not assumed.
- Talking is not assumed.
- The body becomes the first crime scene.

### Chapter 3 — The World Has Changed

- AI and the internet become part of the new world Steven must understand.
- Steven meets the technological gap not as a gimmick but as survival terrain.
- AI is not magic. AI is a tool that must be interrogated.

### Chapter 4 — First Recursion

- A memory returns.
- It does not solve the story.
- It makes the story harder.
- Steven must decide whether memory, body, or record is more reliable.

### Chapter 5 — Due Diligence Begins

- Steven starts checking everything.
- Dates.
- names already known to him.
- accounts.
- messages.
- physical surroundings.
- medical explanations.
- legal definitions.
- maps.
- timelines.

### Chapter 6 — The Ongoing Kidnap Condition

- Steven begins to understand this is not merely something that happened in the past.
- The condition is ongoing.
- The crushing is systemic, physical, legal, social, technological, and neurological.

### Chapter 7 — The First Declaration

- Steven makes the first formal declaration in his own words.
- This can become the first “writ” moment.
- The book announces its function: not entertainment first, but survival documentation.
