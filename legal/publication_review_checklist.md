# Public Publication Review Checklist

Use before publishing or promoting public-facing material.

## 1. Source Integrity

- [ ] Does every factual claim have a source ID or author-declared status?
- [ ] Are direct quotes preserved accurately?
- [ ] Are summaries free of new invented facts?
- [ ] Are unknowns marked `[UNKNOWN — SOURCE DOES NOT PROVIDE THIS]`?
- [ ] Are verification needs marked `[VERIFY]`?

## 2. Legal Risk

- [ ] Are living people named only when necessary?
- [ ] Are allegations framed as statements/allegations unless adjudicated or verified?
- [ ] Are legal conclusions avoided unless reviewed?
- [ ] Are public naming risks logged in `entity_risk_ledger.csv`?
- [ ] Is attorney review required?

## 3. Medical Risk

- [ ] Are medical conclusions avoided unless source-supported?
- [ ] Are symptoms distinguished from diagnoses?
- [ ] Are medication/medical claims marked for review when necessary?
- [ ] Are disability/body descriptions respectful and source-grounded?

## 4. Author-Control

- [ ] Does the wording preserve Steven’s voice?
- [ ] Has AI invented anything?
- [ ] Are contradictions preserved where meaningful?
- [ ] Does the piece maintain Steven’s perspective?

## 5. Public/Private Boundary

- [ ] Is this safe for public GitHub Pages?
- [ ] Does any section belong in private raw archive only?
- [ ] Are sensitive documents redacted where needed?
- [ ] Is CEO sign-off required?
