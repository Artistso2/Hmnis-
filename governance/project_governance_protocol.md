# Project Governance Protocol

Project: **Hello, My Name is Steven: The Lived Experience of Habeas Corpus**  
Author: **Steven Owens / @soquarky / soquarky.click**  
Primary lived location context: **Ocean Shores, Washington 98569**  
Default time context: **Pacific Time / America/Los_Angeles**

---

## 1. Non-Assumption Rule

No assumption becomes project canon.

If information is not provided by Steven or a source document, mark it:

```text
[UNKNOWN — STEVEN HAS NOT PROVIDED THIS]
```

If information is present but needs confirmation, mark it:

```text
[VERIFY]
```

If information is extracted by AI, it is not automatically true. It is a candidate until Steven approves it.

---

## 2. Voice Rule

The private/raw manuscript source is first-person Steven perspective.

This includes:

- Steven’s words,
- Steven’s dictations,
- Steven’s statements,
- Steven’s chronology,
- Steven’s body evidence,
- Steven’s WTF / recursive review of past material,
- Steven’s perspective from a state of amnesia and recovery.

The book may preserve certainty, confusion, contradiction, anger, humor, horror, tenderness, and discovery as they appear in Steven’s voice.

The editorial system may organize that voice. It may not replace it.

---

## 3. Amnesia / Recursion Rule

These documents and AI chats may come from different phases of amnesia, awakening, recursive memory return, or due diligence.

Therefore, contradictions are not automatically errors.

They may be:

- memory recursion,
- date conflict,
- source conflict,
- language evolution,
- medical/neurological uncertainty,
- legal/evidentiary gap,
- or a narrative pressure point.

Do not smooth contradictions without review.

---

## 4. Timestamp Rule

Use **Pacific Time / America/Los_Angeles** as the primary lived-time standard because Steven is in Ocean Shores, Washington 98569.

For technical/legal traceability, logs should also preserve UTC when generated by scripts.

Preferred fields:

```text
created_at_pacific
created_at_utc
source_time_text
source_timezone_if_known
processed_at_pacific
processed_at_utc
```

If the source says “PST,” preserve that wording as the source wording. Internally, use `America/Los_Angeles` to handle Pacific time consistently.

---

## 5. Legal / Attorney-Ready Rule

This project should be rigorous enough to produce attorney-ready packets.

The system must track:

- statement,
- source anchor,
- evidence label,
- verification status,
- public naming risk,
- legal review flag,
- medical review flag,
- living-person risk,
- publication status,
- and next verification step.

The project files are not legal advice. They are attorney-ready editorial and evidentiary organization.

---

## 6. Decision Protocol

For meaningful decisions, present options rather than assuming.

Default option pattern:

1. **Safest option**
2. **Boldest option**
3. **Hybrid / recommended option**

For major public-facing or legally sensitive changes, log the decision in:

```text
project_log/decision_log.csv
```

---

## 7. CEO Sign-Off Threshold

Steven does not need to approve every routine implementation.

Steven should be asked before changes that are:

- legally sensitive,
- major public claims,
- major title/subtitle changes,
- major identity framing,
- new public naming of living people,
- medical/legal conclusions,
- irreversible restructuring,
- or anything that would reasonably come across Steven’s desk as CEO for sign-off.

---

## 8. Public / Private Distinction

Private raw material may preserve first-person intensity and complete source truth.

Public material should be curated for:

- source integrity,
- privacy,
- defamation risk,
- living-person risk,
- medical/legal precision,
- and reader comprehension.

Public curation must not erase Steven’s voice.
